ABOUT FILM

‘Eat, work, sleep and repeat’, some saying in the past has become a reality in 2020. But the world still must move on. Why not keep a positive attitude while we are at it? Here is a rendition of how some of us here at NYU Abu Dhabi perceive the quarantine life. With tons of assignments clustering up our desks (I hope my professors do read this), the idea that we want to project with this short video is that mental peace comes down to finding fun in the small things in life. In these events, mental health is of utmost importance. So, whether it is binge watching series or talking hours with your loved ones, just do it (NIKE please sponsor us) as one’s happiness is of extreme importance.

Meet us! The spatula, a high-spirited soul, spreading happiness and laughter around with its jolliness. It is always good to have such nice people around, isn’t it? As new semester starts, our Ms. Tea got a lot to soak in. The apple may belong to Group 8 of periodic table as she is an introvert ,but she has her own ways of having fun after a hectic routine. Meet our a bit funky Mr. Banana. We promise that you will be fond of him.
